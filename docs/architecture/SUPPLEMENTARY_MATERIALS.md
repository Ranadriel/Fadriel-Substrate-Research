### Why It Belongs in the Patent Package

The qu-septit substrate rests on a specific philosophical claim: **absence is a state of being**, not a null pointer. `Ideas_Register.md #2` formalizes this — *"Zero does not exist in this substrate. Absence is represented by the lowest state, not by null."* The failure-vector map from the golden equation operates on the same principle — forbidden couplings are not blocked, they are **structurally absent**. Dark matter inference, electron hole particles, cavitation bubbles, and event-horizon shadows are all instances of the same physics that the qu-septit encodes as a first-class computational primitive.

*The Mad Science of Nothing* is the public-facing philosophical companion: it demonstrates, in accessible and interactive form, the author's facility with the "nothing as identity" principle that underlies the patent filings. Useful as a supplementary packet for:

- Patent counsel (context for Filing 1's qu-septit novelty claim)
- Investors (evidence that the technical architecture emerges from a coherent worldview, not ad-hoc engineering)
- Academic collaborators (demonstrates the theoretical grounding of the substrate choice)
