## What it is

The Fadriel Cognitive Engine is a deterministic artificial cognitive system built from first principles in physics, chemistry, and neuroscience. It is not a neural network and does not perform statistical generation; cognition, memory, emotion, reasoning, and speech are deterministic outputs of wave-physics simulation over a novel computational substrate. A neural language model is optionally integrated as a peripheral output enricher, never as an author. The engine has been under sole-inventor development since 2022 and is currently being evaluated against the ARC-AGI abstract-reasoning benchmark privately, without neural-network training.

## What it solves

The architecture answers four open problems in contemporary artificial intelligence at the substrate level — not as policy layers added on top of an otherwise unconstrained model.

- **Hallucination.** Fabrication is prevented by the substrate itself, not by a downstream classifier. When the physical precondition for producing a concept is absent, the linguistic pipeline is suppressed; the system cannot assemble a lie because the material is not there.
- **Energy cost.** Information is held in superposition through intermediate computation and crystallised only at the actuator boundary. The Landauer tax of repeated erasure is abolished except where physical work is emitted. Idle draw measured on a consumer laptop is five to fifteen watts.
- **Grounding.** The self-model is constructed by introspecting the system's own source code and directory topology, encoded as a live knowledge graph that updates whenever the implementation changes. Grounding is the computational truth of the system's current state, not an authored claim.
- **Self-reference.** Exploited rather than avoided. The architecture's correctness criterion is convergence on φ, the fixed point of any system that contains itself; when the substrate's compiler emits a binary byte-identical to the binary that just compiled it, the architecture has landed on its own attractor.

## How it works

The premise underneath the architecture is that the universe is already computing at its quantum substrate; the engineering question is not how to force electrons into definite states but where to place the observation point so that the substrate's pre-existing computation resolves into the desired answer. Information is free; the only thermodynamic cost is erasure.

The substrate unit is the **qu-septit** — a computational element holding seven physical wave properties (energy, phase, spin, charge, coherence, coupling, observer) in one of seven named states, with C(7,2) = 21 pairwise interactions evaluated as a simultaneous N-body problem on every heartbeat. Sensory substrates use lower bases derived from the irreducible modality count of each sense (qu-trit for hearing, qu-quatrit for vision, qu-pentit for taste, qu-hexit for touch, qu-octit for smell); the brain alone uses base 7, which is coprime with every sensory base and so geometrically orthogonal to the peripheral plane. This is the **qu-N-trit scaling law**.

The **golden equation** — five fusion equations plus a recursive feedback term, written `(1+2=3)(2+3=4)(3+4+1=5)(2+4+5=6)(1+3+5+6=7)/7` — governs which inter-layer couplings exist among the seven cognitive layers and absents the rest. Sixty percent of the all-to-all coupling space is structurally forbidden rather than computationally suppressed. The /7 term feeds the observer layer's awareness back into the substrate's potential wells on every cycle, making the medium of cognition self-modifying.

The **recurve bow** is the equation in wave form: seven anchor points iterate under a self-coupling wave equation, and when the system reaches equilibrium the convergence point D settles at G/φ² without instruction. φ does not appear in the source; it appears at the fixed point because the architecture is correctly self-referential. **Failure-vector aiming** is the method by which the system reasons: declare what is impossible, fire into the remainder.

The substrate is laid out as a **city**, not a script. Six sensory "seas" are organised around a perpendicular brain sea; nodes are citizens with mailbox-then-door access; cars carry strands of context along reference graphs; cascades crystallise into permanent paths on success. The **ZestC** programming language compiles to this layout under wave-physics semantics rather than sequential instruction execution.

Three independent constructions — the recurve bow equation, a clean-room secondary project built without reference to the engine, and a comparative ethogram of *Homo sapiens* assembled from contemporary biology — have each rediscovered φ unbidden, confirming that the architecture is tuned to a real attractor rather than to an invented one.

## What is distinctive

Three properties separate the architecture from existing systems.

First, it is not a neural network. Cognition, reasoning, memory, emotion, and speech are deterministic outputs of substrate wave-physics. Where a neural language model is integrated at all, it is an enricher of surface phrasing whose output is rejected if it fails validation against the substrate's authored intent.

Second, the body is load-bearing rather than decorative. Eight simulated neurochemicals modulate cognitive output deterministically; emotion is the threshold-crossing of organ-specific chemistry; heart rate is an emergent output, not a set value; somatic freeze is a real mechanism that the rest of the cognitive pipeline observably responds to.

Third, self-reference is exploited as the correctness criterion. When the substrate's compiler produces a byte-identical version of itself, the architecture has landed on its own fixed point — the physics declares the computation correct without recourse to an external test suite.
