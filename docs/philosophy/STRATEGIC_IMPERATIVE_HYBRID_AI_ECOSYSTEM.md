## THE CRISIS: Why Monolithic AI Fails the Creative Industry
-   Diffusion-only editing causes full image redraws and Identity Drift.
-   Cannot isolate pixel regions; produces artifacts and forbidden features.
-   Blocks adoption by AAA studios, VFX houses, and major IP owners.

## THE SOLUTION: The Hybrid Editing Architecture
-   **Stage 1: Diffusion** — Initial creation and concept generation.
-   **Stage 2: Tool-Based Engine** — Surgical, pixel-accurate, non-destructive edits.
