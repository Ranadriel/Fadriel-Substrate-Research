## How to Use This Report

1. **🔴 HIGH results** — send to patent counsel immediately with the matched kill terms. These are the closest known prior art. Counsel must distinguish your invention from these in the claims.
2. **🟡 MODERATE results** — review each abstract. If the mechanism is different (even if the goal is similar), document why it's distinguishable. Feed this to counsel as your own prior art analysis.
3. **🟢 LOW results** — file for reference. Low threat but good to have in your prosecution history as evidence you performed a search.
4. **Re-run this demon** before each provisional is filed, and again before each non-provisional is filed. Prior art emerges continuously — a paper published today could change everything.
